SECOND SAMUEL
Logline
"Everybody's got a secret."
Summary
The small town of Second Samuel is plunged into a stir of commotion and self-rumination by the passing of the "sweetest woman whatever drawed a breath!" For 20 years, Miss Gertrude has helped the sick, introduced lovers, taught piano lessons, even played piano for the church choir, and ever'body loves her apple pie. What happens when the whole town finds out her secret?
